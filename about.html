About Younique Apps LLC

The company behind ISeeYouSeeMe (ICUCMe).

Who We Are

Younique Apps LLC is a single-member limited liability company formed in the State of Alaska in 2026. We build privacy-first software for personal device security. ISeeYouSeeMe (ICUCMe) is our first product.

Younique Apps LLC is owner-operated. The owner is a nearly 30-year Alaska Resident and father of three intelligent and beautiful Alaskan daughters and three strong and adventurous Alaskan sons. The company was formed because a small, independent business answers to its customers alone — not to investors, not to data brokers, not to advertisers.

What We Believe

We believe your personal data belongs to you and stays on your device.

Younique Apps LLC operates no server. We do not see, store, or process any photo, location, screenshot, or activity log captured by ISeeYouSeeMe. The optional Cloud Backup feature uses your own personal cloud storage account — never ours — and the backup file is encrypted on your device before it ever leaves the phone. You hold the only key.

We believe in being a real, accountable company. We are formed under Alaska law. We pay Alaska taxes. We file our paperwork on time. When you have a problem with the application, the person who responds to your email is the same person who built the application. There is no outsourced support center, no overseas call queue, and no automated reply system that wastes your time.

A small, independent, Alaska-formed company building privacy-first software for the people who use it — not for advertisers, not for data brokers, not for investors.

Where We Are Going

ISeeYouSeeMe is the first product released by Younique Apps LLC. The company has a slate of additional privacy-first applications under development, each guided by the same principles: local-only data storage as the default, encryption performed on the user's device, no servers operated by Younique Apps LLC, and no transmission of user data without explicit user consent.
